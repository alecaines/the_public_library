Algebra Syllabus



The goal of these modules will be to give you a sense for the underlying mechanisms of the number systems we use in our day-to-day lives as mathematicians or mathematically inclined scholars. There will be a strong emphasis on proof, and being able to argue for or against the onjectures made by peers or the problems in the book. That being said, most--if not all--of the work you will do will involve proof. If you'd like to know more about proof, scroll down to the end of this document!

It is recommended that you read the lessons carefully before approaching the exercsies. Please reach out to alecaines at email dot com for anyt questions you may have about the lessons or the exercises!

While meeting every other week would be best for motivation, we all have busy schedules and thus may need to be a bit more impromptu about when we meet. So long as we do our best to make it through a chapter each month, I'm confident we won't feel as if we're stagnating. The dates in parentehse are suggested meeting times! We can decide where we want to meet on the day leading up to the meeting!

If you'd like to make any amendments or suggestions to the syllabus, please let me know!
